About Ekarikthin 2024

Experience the heartbeat of innovation and culture at Ekarikthin, the crown jewel of techno-cultural extravaganzas! Hosted by the National Institute of Technology Nagaland in the picturesque north-east of India, this two-days spectacle is more than just a festival—it's a celebration of talent and creativity. Ekarikthin stands as the grandest stage for the youth of India, uniting bright minds from every corner in a symphony of cultural and technical brilliance. Join the rhythm, ignite your passion, and be part of the largest techno-cultural fiesta in Nagaland!

Join us in celebrating the power of youth, where every moment is a chance to shine in the spotlight of cultural, technical, and sports excellence!

EXCITING EVENTS FOR EVERYONE

Embark on a cultural odyssey at Ekarikthin, where the spotlight awaits your brilliance! From the rhythmic beats of music to the mesmerizing dance floor, the eloquence of literary pursuits to the thrill of quizzes, the dramatic stage to the finesse of fine arts and the magic of films—Ekarikthin is your canvas for expression. Join us in this spectacular showcase of talent, where participants from across colleges vie for laurels, turning each event into a masterpiece of skill and passion! Your moment in the cultural limelight begins here!
